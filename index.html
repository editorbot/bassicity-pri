Privacy Policy for HandyKit

Effective Date: February 8, 2025

Last Updated: February 8, 2025

Introduction

HandyKit ("we," "our," or "the app") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how our mobile application collects, uses, and safeguards your information.

HandyKit is a utility application that provides the following features:

  • Flashlight
  • Calculator (Basic & Scientific)
  • Notes
  • Tic-Tac-Toe Game

Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally on Your Device

HandyKit stores the following data locally on your device only:

  1. Notes Data: When you create notes, the title, content, color, and timestamps are stored locally using your device's storage.
  2. Game Statistics: Your Tic-Tac-Toe game wins, losses, and draws are stored locally.
  3. App Preferences: Basic app settings and preferences.

Important: All data is stored locally on your device. We do NOT collect, transmit, or store any of your data on external servers or in the cloud.

Permissions

The app requests the following permissions:

  • Camera/Flashlight Permission (Android: CAMERA, FLASHLIGHT): Required only for the flashlight feature to control your device's flash/torch.
  • Storage Permission: To save and retrieve notes and game statistics locally on your device.

How We Use Your Information

  • Notes: Stored locally for your personal use. You can create, edit, and delete notes at any time.
  • Game Statistics: Stored locally to track your game progress.
  • Flashlight: No data is collected. The camera permission is only used to control the flashlight hardware.
  • Calculator: No data is collected or stored.

Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do NOT:

  • Collect any personal information
  • Share your data with third parties
  • Use analytics or tracking services
  • Display advertisements
  • Transmit data to external servers
  • Require user accounts or login credentials

All data remains 100% on your device.

Data Security

Since all data is stored locally on your device:

  • Your data is as secure as your device
  • We recommend using device lock screens and encryption
  • If you uninstall the app, all locally stored data (notes and game stats) will be permanently deleted

Children's Privacy

HandyKit does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13. The app is suitable for all ages and does not require any personal information.

Data Retention and Deletion

  • Notes: You can delete individual notes at any time using the delete button in the app.
  • Game Statistics: You can reset all game statistics using the "Reset Statistics" button in the game screen.
  • Complete Data Removal: Uninstalling the app will permanently delete all locally stored data.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes indicates acceptance of the updated policy.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (all data is visible within the app)
  • Delete your data (through the app's delete functions)
  • Control app permissions (through your device settings)
